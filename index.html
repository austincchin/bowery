What's New?

We just completed a successful summer at Mont Lawn Summer Camp!

Children from struggling families and tough neighborhoods created exciting memories & relationships that will last a lifetime. Besides the fun parts of camp (such as rock climbing, archery, and swimming!), many campers have grown to make better choices in life. Sometimes being with other children for an entire week can create conflict, but it is also a learning opportunity for kids to practice self-control, patience and communication skills — small steps that make a big impact.

Amari, Mont Lawn '14

"I used to think that you have to fight to get what you want," Amari says. Living in a tough neigborhood, Amani grew up constantly on guard, ready to jump to violence.

Then he heard about Mont Lawn Camp and spent a week there in the summer. He loved the adventures - swimming, canoeing, sleeping in a tent, playing soccer and football with his friends. But more than that, he learned a different way to live. The counselors listened to him and understood him. "They taught me about being a leader and how to speak up for others," he said.

"Going to Mont Lawn Camp has taught me that violence is not the best answer. Intead of risking my life in a fight, I know I am strong enough to walk away." Amari now goes to our City Camp every week during the school year and has friends who support and encourage him. He wants to get a college degree and open his own business. "A lot of people from camp believe in me, so I know I can do it."
